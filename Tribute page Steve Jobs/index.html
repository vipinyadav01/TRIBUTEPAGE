Steve Jobs

Steve Jobs, founder of Apple

"Your time is limited, so don't waste it living someone else's life."

- Steve Jobs

Here's a timeline of Steve Jobs life

24 feb, 1955

Born in San Francisco as Abdul Latif Jandali to Abdulfattah Jandali and Joanne Carole Schieble, and was adopted by Paul and Clara Jobs

1961

The family move to Mountain View

1969

Steve Jobs meet Steve Wozniak through a mutual friend

1972

Steve and Woz build and send an illegally blue boxes that allow to make phone calls for free

1976

Jobs, Wozniak and Ronald Wayne found Apple Computer in the garage of Jobs’s Los Altos home and present the Apple I

1977

Jobs and Wozniak introduced the Apple II : it was one of the first highly successful mass-produced microcomputer products in the world

1985

Following his resignation from Apple, Jobs found NeXT Inc

1997

Apple buy NeXT for $427 million, bringing Jobs back to the company he had cofounded

24 aug, 2011

Jobs announce his resignation as Apple’s CEO due to his cancer. He named Tim Cook as his successor.

5 oct, 2011

Jobs died at his Palo Alto home
